Services offered at Teleposta Guidance and Counseling Organization

1. Therapy

We offer both in-person and virtual therapy sessions tailored to your comfort and convenience. Our licensed therapists work with you at your own pace, helping you navigate personal challenges, emotional difficulties, and mental health concerns. Whether you prefer to meet face-to-face or from the comfort of your home, both options deliver the same high standard of professional care.

2. Counseling

Our counseling services provide a safe and confidential space where you can openly discuss what is on your mind. Our experienced counselors guide you through life's difficulties — whether related to relationships, stress, grief, career, or personal growth. Through structured sessions, we help you develop practical coping strategies and build a clearer path forward.
